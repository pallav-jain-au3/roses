Why do we love roses?

Rose is one of the most favorite and best known plants in the world. It can be found all over the world.

There are more than 360 different species of roses.

Rose flowers can reach from couple to 33 inches in diameter.

Roses can be white, yellow, pink and red colored and there are various shades of these colors.
